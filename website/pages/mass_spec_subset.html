Mass Spectrometry Subset of EVODEX-M

This table lists a subset of EVODEX-M operators specifically curated for mass spectrometry applications. The subset is derived from partial reactions where either the substrate or product is a single molecule. By adding the provided masses to known substrate peaks, a superset of all potential product mass peaks of the same ion type can be predicted. This approach aids in identifying possible products from given substrates in enzymatic transformations.
